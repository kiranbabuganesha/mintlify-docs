---
title: Filters Glossary
---

The search API includes various filters that can be used for querying and retrieving specific information.

|Filter Name            |Filter Key             | Filter Data                                                                                                                    |
|-----------------------|-----------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Company               | company                | **includes**: `id1`, `id2`<br /><br /> **excludes**: `id1`, `id2`                                                                             |
| Location              | location              | **includes**: `Label1`, `Label2`<br /><br /> **excludes**: `Label1`, `Label2`                                                                 |
| Industry              | industry              | **includes**: `id1`, `id2`<br /><br /> **excludes**: `id1`, `id2`                                                                             |
| Company Keyword       | company_keyword        | **includes**: `Label1`, `Label2`<br /><br /> **excludes**: `Label1`, `Label2`<br /><br /> **includeAll**: `Label1`, `Label2`                             |
| Revenue               | revenue               | **min**: 10, **max**: 100                                                                                                              |
| Total Employee Count  | total_employee_count    | **min**: 10, **max**: 100                                                                                                              |
| Employee By Dept.     | employee_by_department  | **finance**: \{ min: 10, max: 100 }<br /><br /> **hr**: \{ min: 10, max: 100 }                                                                       |
| Buying Intent         | buying_intent          | **intentScores**: `high`,`low`,`medium`,`none`<br /><br /> **intentTopics**: `id1`, `id2`<br /><br /> **timeFrame**: `yesterday`                          |
| Technology            | technology            | **includes**: `id1`, `id2`<br /><br /> **includeAll**: `id1`, `id2`                                                                           |
| Funding               | funding               | **stages**: `id1`, `id2`<br /><br /> **lastFundingDate**: \{ min: 10, max: 100 }<br /><br /> **lastFundingAmount**: \{ min: 10, max: 100 }                    |
| Founded Year          | founded_year           | **yearRange**: \{ min: 10, max: 100 }<br /><br /> **includeUnknown**: true                                                                         |
| Job Opening           | job_opening            | **jobTitles**: `Label1`, `Label2`<br /><br /> **jobLocations**: `Label1`, `Label2`<br /><br /> **jobOpenCurrently**: \{ min: 10, max: 100 }<br /><br /> **jobOpenedTimeFrame**: `Last 7 days` |
| Headcount Growth      | head_count_growth       | **growthRange**: \{ min: 10, max: 100 }<br /><br /> **timeFrame**: `3`<br /><br /> **department**: `label`                                                      |
| Retail Location       | retail_location        | **min**: 10, **max**: 100                                                                                                              |
